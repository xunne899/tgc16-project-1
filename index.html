About Recycling

  • Reconsidering something that has no value and to be thrown away
  • Responsible for production, consumption, reuse, and recovery of products, without posing threat to the environment or human health
  • Able to blend recycled waste harmlessly back to the surroundings

The 3Rs

  • Save the enviroment by implementing Reduce, Reuse, Recycle
  • Reduce by cutting down the usage of product at the start
  • Reuse by reusing waste for another purpose
  • Recycle by sending waste for additional re-processing work to form a new product
